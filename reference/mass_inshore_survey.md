# Massachusetts inshore bottom trawl survey

Data include aggregated time series of inshore fishery-independent trawl
survey data from Massachusetts waters. MA inshore surveys have been
performed biannually in the spring and fall since 1978.

## Usage

``` r
mass_inshore_survey
```

## Format

880 rows and 5 columns

- Var: Specifies variable type, including SOE species groupings and
  sampling season with stratified mean biomass per tow

- Value: Value of variable `Var`.

- Time: Year.

- Units: Units of variable `Var`.

- EPU: Ecological Production Unit. These data reflect inshore sampling
  in MA state waters.

## Details

Technical details related to the MA inshore survey are available at
<https://www.mass.gov/files/documents/2016/08/tm/tr-38.pdf>.
