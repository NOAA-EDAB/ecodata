# Northeast Channel slopewater proportions

Percent total of water type observed in the deep Northeast Channel
(150-200 m water depth).

## Usage

``` r
slopewater
```

## Format

90 rows and 5 columns

- Var: Specifies slopewater source (either Labrador Slope Water (LSLW)
  or Warm Slope Water (WSW))

- Value: Proportion of slopewater derived from distinct source.

- Units: Units of variable `Var`; proportion.

- Time: Year.

- EPU: Ecological Production Unit where data originated.

## Details

Read more about the roles of different slopewater types in the Gulf of
Maine at
<https://www.integratedecosystemassessment.noaa.gov/regions/northeast/components/other-physical-drivers>.
