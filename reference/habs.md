# Harmful Algal Blooms - Alexandrium and Paralitic Shellfish Poisoning

Annual time series of the Paralytic Shellfish Poisoning (PSP) and
Alexandrium toxins in the Gulf of Maine.

## Usage

``` r
habs
```

## Format

Time series with 155 rows and 5 columns

- Var: Regions of interest.

- Source: Harmful bloom source (PSP or Alexandrium).

- Value: Value of variable `Var`.

- EPU: Representative Ecological Production Unit (MAB = Mid-Atlantic
  Bight, GB = Georges Bank, GOM = Gulf of Maine, SS = Scotian Shelf, or
  All).

- Time: Years.
