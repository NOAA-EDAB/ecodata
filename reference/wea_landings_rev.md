# Fisheries maximum and minimum landings and revenue from wind energy areas (WEAs).

Data include top ten species Landings and Revenue from Wind Energy
Areas.

## Usage

``` r
wea_landings_rev
```

## Format

Data set contains 32 rows and 5 columns

- NEFMC, MAFMC, and ASMFC Managed Species: Managed Fishery.

- perc_landings_max: Max Landings Percent from WEA.

- perc_revenue_max: Max Revenue Percent from WEA.

- Units: Units for landings or revenue.

- Council: New England (NEFMC) or Mid Atlantic (MAFMC)

## Details

More information including processing and indicator derivation steps are
available at
